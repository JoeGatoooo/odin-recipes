How to Make Microwaveable Popcorn

Description

Popcorn is a must have when it comes to snakcs to watch during a movie. Luckily, the invention of the microwave has made popcorn extremely easy to make.

Ingredients

  • A bowl
  • A microwave
  • A package of any branded microwaveable popcorn

Steps

  1. Remove film from the popcorn package
  2. Read the packaging to figure out what way to insert the package into the microwave and how long it should be in there.
  3. Wait for the mircowave to beep
  4. empty the popcorn into a bowl and enjoy!
