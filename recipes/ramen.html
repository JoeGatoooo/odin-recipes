How to Make Instant Ramen

Description

Instant ramen in the poor college student essential when it comes to cheap and unnutritious food. It comes in a wide variety of flavors and packaging, so be sure to select the best looking one and follow this "recipe"!

Ingredients

  • A bowl
  • A stovetop
  • A package of any instant ramen of your choosing (Maruchan for the most successful results)
  • A fork
  • A pot filled with water

Steps

  1. Remove and serparate the ramen and flavor packet from packaging.
  2. Turn stove on to high heat.
  3. Place the pot with water onto the burning stovetop and wait for water to boil.
  4. Once boiling, place ramen block into water and break apart using fork.
  5. Wait for noodles to cook to your liking. Depending whether or not you like broth, you may want to strain the water out before placing noodles into a bowl.
  6. Once noodles are in a bowl, open flavor packet and pour seasoning onto the noodles and stir with a fork.
  7. Enjoy 75% of your daily sodium intake in a single meal!
