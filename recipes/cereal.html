How to Pour a Bowl of Cereal

Description

Cereal has been a breakfast custom for over a century, so it is good to know how to pour a good bowl of cereal. Following the information below will tell you how.

Ingredients

  • A bowl
  • A spoon
  • Cereal(s) of your choosing
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Pour the cereal(s) you chose into the bowl
  2. Add milk. Be sure not to submurge the cereal completely
  3. Grab your spoon and enjoy!
